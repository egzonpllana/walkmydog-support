# Walk My Dog Planner

Plan walks, track routines, check safe foods, and keep your dog healthy with AI-powered scheduling.

## Features

- **Dog Profiles** — Manage detailed profiles for each of your dogs including breed, weight, health status, and more.
- **Walk Scheduling** — Create recurring walk schedules with reminders and alarms. Track walk sessions in real time.
- **AI Walk Planner** — Get personalized walk plans based on your dog's breed, age, and goals like weight management or anxiety relief.
- **Safe Foods Guide** — Searchable database of foods with safety ratings, allergy warnings, and breed-specific guidance.
- **iCloud Sync** — Keep your data synced across all your Apple devices with encrypted iCloud backup.
- **Live Activities** — Track your active walk right from the lock screen with real-time duration updates.
