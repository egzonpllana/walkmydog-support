# Support

We're here to help you and your dog.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add a new dog?
Go to the "My Dogs" tab and tap the add button. Fill in your dog's details including name, breed, date of birth, and other optional information like weight and collar color.

### How do I schedule a walk?
Go to the "Walks" tab and create a new walk schedule. Set the time, duration, and which days of the week you'd like to walk. You can enable notifications and alarms to remind you.

### What is the AI Walk Planner?
The AI Walk Planner (premium feature) generates a personalized walk plan for your dog based on their breed, age, and your goals (e.g., weight management, anxiety relief, socialization). It recommends optimal walk times, durations, and activities. **All AI-generated plans are suggestions for informational purposes only and are not medical or veterinary advice.** Always consult your veterinarian before changing your dog's exercise routine.

### How does food safety work?
The "Safe Foods" tab provides a searchable database of foods categorized as Safe, Caution, or Dangerous for dogs. If you have dog profiles set up, it also provides breed-specific and allergy-specific warnings. **Food safety ratings are general suggestions, not veterinary advice.** Always consult your vet before introducing new foods, especially if your dog has allergies or health conditions.

### How do I restore my purchases?
Go to the Hub tab, tap on your subscription section, and use the "Restore Purchases" option. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

### How does iCloud Sync work?
iCloud Sync (premium feature) keeps your dog profiles and walk schedules synchronized across all your Apple devices signed in with the same Apple ID. Enable it from Hub > iCloud Sync. Your data is encrypted and stored in your private iCloud container.

### Can I recover a deleted dog profile?
Yes. Deleted dogs are soft-deleted and can be recovered from Hub > Deleted Items.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel your Walk My Dog Planner subscription.
